Friction forces

Frictional force occurs when bodies move or when trying to move them from their place.

It acts on the surface of bodies and makes it difficult for them to move relative to each other.

Refers to the forces of an electromagnetic nature.

Friction is dry and liquid.

Dry friction is divided into three types: static friction, sliding friction and rolling friction.

Sliding friction occurs when one body slides over the surface of another.
