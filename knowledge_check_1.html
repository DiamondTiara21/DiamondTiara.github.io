Knowledge check (Kinematics)

 

Knowledge check

1.     Kinematics is a branch of theoretical mechanics that studies:

a)     mechanical motion of material solids and their interaction

b)     conditions of equilibrium of bodies under the action of forces

c)     the movement of bodies as movement in space; the characteristics of bodies and the causes of movement are not considered

d)     movement of bodies under the action of forces

2.     The distance between the start and end points is:

a)     path

b)     distance travelled

c)     offset

d)     trajectory

3.     The velocity vector of a point of a rotating body is always directed...

a)     normal to the trajectory

b)     from the center of rotation

c)     tangent to the trajectory

d)     to the center of rotation

4.     The trajectory is called...

a)     a line in space described by a point when moving

b)     a vector connecting the starting and ending positions of a point

c)     path length

d)     vector connecting the origin and the end point of the path

5.     Tangential acceleration characterizes...

a)     speed change in direction

b)     speed of movement along the trajectory

c)     the direction of movement along the trajectory

d)     change in speed by amount

6.     In translational motion, all points of a solid body...

a)     have the same trajectories, speeds and accelerations

b)     have the same trajectories, velocities and tangential accelerations

c)     have the same speeds and normal accelerations

d)     move evenly and rectilinearly

7.     If a point passes equal segments of the path in equal intervals of time, then movement...

a)     uniform

b)     uneven

c)     straight forward

d)     curved

e)     missing

8.     Establish a correspondence between physical quantities and their units of measurement

a)     Velocity                                              1) s

b)     Acceleration                                      2) m/s

c)     Distance travelled                            3) m/s2

d)     Time                                                    4) m

9.     The length of the line that the material point outlines when moving in space is called...

a)     speed

b)     acceleration

c)     trajectory

d)     path

a)     BC
